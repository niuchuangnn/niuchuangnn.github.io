Hi

I am a Postdoc and serve as an Associate Director in AXIS Lab led by Prof. Ge Wang at Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute . I received my B.S. in biomedical engineering in 2015, and my Ph.D. in pattern recognition and machine intelligence in 2020, from Xidian University . I was a visiting student at Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute from 2019 to 2020.

My research interest is in unsupervised/self-supervised learning, weakly-supervised learning, representation learning, clustering, and biomedical imaging and analysis.
